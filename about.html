About Us

Equiblue bridges European innovation and global field realities. We specialize in developing carbon projects in agriculture, livestock, and nature-based systems — making them financially viable and accessible.

Who We Are

Founded in Estonia, we leverage Europe’s funding networks, research ecosystems, and climate policies, while testing and scaling solutions in India and other emerging markets.

Our Mission

To democratize the carbon economy by reducing entry barriers, building financially sustainable models, and enabling small players to generate revenue from verified carbon credits.

Our Values

Accessibility

Projects should not be blocked by high upfront costs.

Sustainability

Credits must create both climate and community value.

Transparency

Registry-aligned processes that are credible and verifiable.

Collaboration

Working with universities, NGOs, startups, and investors.
