Our Services

End-to-end support to design, register, and scale credible climate projects.

1) Carbon Project Development

  • Concept notes & feasibility studies
  • Baseline & additionality assessments
  • Low-cost Project Design Documents (PDDs)
  • Farmer & cooperative engagement models
  • Registry registration support

2) Monitoring, Reporting & Verification (MRV)

  • IoT, farmer apps & GIS-based monitoring
  • Carbon stock & soil assessments
  • Registry-aligned monitoring protocols
  • Verification readiness documentation

3) Financial Sustainability & Market Linkages

  • Revenue-sharing models for NGOs & cooperatives
  • Connecting projects with credit buyers & corporate offset programs

4) Capacity Building & Knowledge Transfer

  • Training on carbon accounting & project design
  • Workshops for NGOs, startups, and cooperatives
  • Equiblue Academy: long-term training & research platform
